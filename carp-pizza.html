CARP PIZZA

Rebranding for small business. Branding guide created with all guidelines.

Research and a moodboard was the first step. After a style was chosen, sketches or a logo began. Numerous sketches were made to find the best option for the business.

Once a logo is chosen, construction in illustrator is started, and branding boards are made. Then the associated pieces are outlined for the branding guide. Finally the guide is created and organized.
