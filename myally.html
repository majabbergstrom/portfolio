MYALLY

Branding for online presence. Process began with moodboard and many sketches to develop every idea. After selecting the favourites, more sketching was done to perfect the design.

Once a design was chosen, construction in illustrator began, then sketches for other assets, such as banners began.

Final pieces for banners, on-screen graphics, and panels were then built in photoshop and optimized for web.
